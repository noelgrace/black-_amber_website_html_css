Richie Hogan's Player Profile







Richard "Richie" Hogan (born 8 August 1988) is an Irish hurler who plays as a midfielder for the Kilkenny senior team. Born in Danesfort, County Kilkenny, Hogan first excelled at hurling during his schooling at St. Kieran's College. He arrived on the inter-county scene at the age of fifteen when he first linked up with the Kilkenny minor team, before later joining the under-21 side. He joined the senior panel during the 2007 championship. Hogan later became a regular member of the starting fifteen and has since won five All-Ireland medals, four Leinster medals and four National Hurling League medals. He has been an All-Ireland runner-up on one occasion. As a member of the Leinster inter-provincial team, Hogan has won one Railway Cup medal.

Hogan's brother Paddy is also a member of the Kilkenny senior team while his close relation D. J. Carey is regarded as one of the all-time greats of the game.

Richie Hogan played underage up as far as u14 with a neighbouring club team Bennettsbridge before transferring to Danesfort. richie Hogan had gone to national school in Bennetsbridge and his mother is from there. However, once Richie attended St. Kieran's College, scondary school he had to play with Danesfort as he was living there. At club level he is an All-Ireland and Leinster medallist at junior level with Danesfort. In addition to these he has also won several championship medals in different grades. The Danesfort club went from Junior to Senior in Kilkenny in the space of 5 years, 2006-2011
